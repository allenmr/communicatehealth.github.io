Here is another handy bookmarklets for exporting markdown from Box Notes:

  1. Drag the "BoxNote2Markdown" button below into your bookmark bar
  2. Click the "BoxNote2Markdown" bookmark anytime you are on a Box Notes page and want to export the text in markdown format (if it needs to be run twice, it will instruct you to do so)
