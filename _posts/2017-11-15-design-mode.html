Past Developer Tips and Creative Time sessions have covered the use of in-browser DevTools to preview small edits to existing pages. Tracking down the right place to make the change is always a challenge, even for developers.

Here’s an easier option: a bookmarklet that turns all text on the page into editable content.

To use the bookmarklet, drag and drop the button below to your bookmarks bar:

Edit page text

It works in every browser (even some mobile browsers) and uses a javascript snippet that turns on "design mode" document.designmode = 'on'
