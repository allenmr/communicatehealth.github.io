Here are two handy bookmarklets for dealing with Zoom meetings:

:

Copy Zoom link to clipboard

  1. Enter your zoom meeting ID in the text box above
  2. Drag the "Copy Zoom link" button below into your bookmark bar
  3. Click the "Copy Zoom link" bookmark anytime you want to copy your zoom meeting URL to your clipboard

Copy Zoom link

Copy Zoom link to clipboard and launch meeting

  1. Enter your zoom meeting ID in the text box above
  2. Drag the "Copy and launch Zoom" button below into your bookmark bar
  3. Click the "Copy and launch Zoom" bookmark anytime you want to copy your zoom meeting URL to your clipboard and launch the meeting from your browser
